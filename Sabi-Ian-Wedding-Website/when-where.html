When

Saturday, June 12, 2027

Church: 1pm — 2pm

The wedding ceremony will start at 1pm. This will be conducted in Romanian, but a translated version will be available on the day if you would like to follow along. The ceremony will take 45 minutes to 1 hour — please feel free to sit or stand wherever you wish.

Reception: 3pm — 3am

We're delighted to welcome you to our wedding reception at 3pm. For our non-Romanian friends, please note the dinner will be served slowly throughout the night, whilst the wedding cake will be brought out at midnight before the afterparty begins.

Where

Church

Biserica "Adormirea Maicii Domnului"

Built in 1778, this historic Romanian Orthodox church has welcomed generations of families for life's most meaningful moments. Surrounded by the timeless beauty and traditions of Transylvania, it is a place of faith, love, and community — making it the perfect setting for us to begin our marriage together.

Address: Strada Andrei Șaguna nr. 206, Hărman, Romania
